About 👋

Who I Am

I'm Swuei, 3D modeling started out as a hobby for me and it's stuck ever since, I find it calming, and it lets me help server owners out by giving them free custom models so they can put their budget toward more important parts of their server instead.

Everything I make is modeled and textured by hand in Blockbench. I provide a .bbmodel that has the textures included inside.
