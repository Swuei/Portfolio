Frequently Asked Questions

What types of models do you create?

I specialize in creating low-poly Minecraft-style 3D models using Blockbench, with texture resolutions of 16x or 32x. These are typically for Minecraft servers, personal projects, or content creation, including items like NPCs, cosmetics, or decorative builds.

Are your models free to use and can I sell them?

Yes, all my models are free and non-exclusive for community use. You can also sell my models if you wish but, I would appreciate if you link the home page of this website in the resource page.

Do you make models for mods?

Simply put, no I do not. When I got into the modeling space my goal was not to make models for mods but rather for minecraft java servers. That being said if I am interested in your project I may or may not decide to work with you on it

Can I request a custom model?

Absolutely! Use the "Make a Request" form on the homepage to submit your request. I create one model per person, and I may deny or cancel requests at my discretion. Ensure your reference image is hosted on ImgBB.com.

Do you create mob models?

I generally don’t create mob models unless they align with a project I’m particularly interested in. I focus more on static models like NPCs, cosmetics, or decorative items.

How long does it take to receive a requested model and how many can I request?

The timeline varies depending on my schedule and the complexity of the request. If I choose to fulfill your request, I’ll contact you via Discord to discuss details and provide an estimated timeframe. I only make 1 model per person unless if I am particularly interested in your work and decide to make more for you.

Can you help with resource pack setup?

Yes, I can provide a basic resource pack setup for the model I create for you, including sounds, models, and textures, if requested. Contact me directly for any additional support.

How can I contact you for support or inquiries?

You can reach me on Discord (@Swuei) for direct message support, especially for minor fixes to models or general inquiries. Please don’t spam, and I’ll get back to you as soon as possible!
