Privacy Policy

Data Collection

I use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve my website. This includes:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on site
  • Download interactions
  • Device and browser information

Download Tracking

When you download files, we track:

  • Which files are downloaded
  • Timestamp of download
  • Basic user agent information

This helps me understand which resources are most valuable to my users.

Your Choices

You can control what data is collected:

  • Use browser settings to block cookies
  • Opt-out of Google Analytics tracking
  • Disable JavaScript to prevent tracking

I don't collect personally identifiable information without your consent.

Form Data Collection

When you submit a model request form, I collect:

  • Your Discord username
  • Model request details
  • Reference image URL (hosted on ImgBB)
  • Texture size preference
  • Intended use of the model
